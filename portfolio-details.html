YatraSewa – Travel Booking Platform for Nepal

YatraSewa is a full-stack travel booking system designed specifically for Nepal. It allows users to seamlessly book hotels, flights, and rental cars based on their preferences and budget. The platform features filters for hotel types, price ranges, and ratings, along with options to explore top-rated hotels and popular travel destinations. It offers a user-friendly interface, real-time search, and secure booking to enhance the travel planning experience.
